Joaquin Sorolla

Joaquin Sorolla was a Spanish painter that prioritized painting landscapes, portraits, and historical themes.

Accomplishments

  • He exhibited his first large history painting that was acquired by the Spanish Government in 1884, before he was 21.
  • He was known for being able to capture the effects of sunlight very well in his paintings, which is why he's painted shots of the beach.
  • His paintings were exhibited in Munich, Paris, Chicago, Vienna, Venice
